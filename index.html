How co-agency works today

The whole industry runs on WhatsApp groups. And it shows.

Hundreds of invite-only groups. 50–200 agents in each. Every listing dumped as a message. No structure, no search, no memory.

01

You can't get in.

Groups are invite-only and gatekept. New agents starve for years before they network into the right ones for their segment.

02

Listings vanish in minutes.

A great listing posted at 9am is buried by 11am. There's no search. You either saw it live, or you missed it forever.

03

Nothing is structured.

Half the messages are missing the price, the tenure, or the size. You waste hours chasing details over private DM.

Common questions

Things every realtor asks first.

Honest answers, no fine print.

Q · 01

Do I have to leave my WhatsApp groups?

No. BLAST sits next to them. Use it for discovery; keep WhatsApp for the chat that closes the deal. Most founding members run both for the first month, then quietly mute the groups.

Q · 02

Can other agents see my private notes?

Never. Drafts stay private to your account. Only listings you tap "Blast" on go public. Notes, prices you're testing, motivation flags — all yours alone.

Q · 03

What does it cost?

Free to start — 30 credits on signup, +30 every Done Deal. If you close regularly, you'll never need to top up. Casual users can buy a small credit pack.

Q · 04

Is my REN number visible to the public?

Only to other verified agents on BLAST. Never to consumers — BLAST is agent-only by design. We verify your REN/PEA/E-number on signup and gate the network behind it.

Q · 05

What if a deal closes off-platform?

Mark it Done Deal honestly — it benefits you (credits) and the network (real price data). We don't take a cut of any commission, ever. BLAST is not in the closing path.

Q · 06

Is there a desktop version?

Yes — a power-user web app for active closers, with multi-listing management and a Linear-style command palette. Mobile-first, but the desk is covered.
